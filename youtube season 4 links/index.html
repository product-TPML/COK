Season 5

Cuisines of Karnataka

Cuisines of Karnataka returns with its fifth season, celebrating the rich culinary heritage, diverse flavours, and timeless food traditions of Karnataka. Bringing together culture and storytelling, the platform showcases the state's iconic recipes and the people behind them. This season features 15 exclusive recipe videos curated and prepared by renowned celebrity chefs, while introducing the exciting concept of food pairing to highlight combinations that elevate the authentic flavours of Karnataka's regional cuisines. The journey continues right here on this microsite, your destination to explore recipes, watch videos, participate in contests, engage with interactive experiences, and discover the unique tastes that make Karnataka's cuisine truly unforgettable.
